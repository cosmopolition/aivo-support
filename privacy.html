Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 17, 2025

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

AIVO is designed with privacy as a core principle. We believe your personal data belongs to you and you alone.

🔒 We Do Not Collect Your Data

AIVO does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. All your data stays on your device.

How AIVO Works

Local Storage

All your data, including preferences, records, and content you create, is stored locally on your device. We have no access to this information.

iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you choose to enable iCloud sync, your data is synchronized across your devices using Apple's iCloud service. This data is encrypted and stored in your personal iCloud account, managed entirely by Apple. We do not have access to your iCloud data.

What We Don't Do

  • ❌ We do not collect personal information
  • ❌ We do not track your location
  • ❌ We do not use analytics or tracking tools
  • ❌ We do not share data with third parties
  • ❌ We do not display advertisements

Third-Party Services

AIVO uses Apple's iCloud for optional data synchronization. Apple's privacy policy governs how your iCloud data is handled. You can review Apple's Privacy Policy for more information.

Your Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Delete the app to remove all local data
  • Disable iCloud sync in your device settings
  • Manage your iCloud data through Apple's settings

Children's Privacy

Since we do not collect any personal data, AIVO is safe for users of all ages.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
