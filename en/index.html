Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?

Open the app and tap "Get Started". You can sign up using your email or Apple ID.

How do I restore my subscription?

Go to Settings → Account → Restore Purchases. Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

How can I delete my account?

Go to Settings → Account → Delete Account. Please note this action is irreversible.

The app is not working properly. What should I do?

Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, please contact us with details about your device and the problem you're experiencing.
